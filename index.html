Our Post-Construction Cleaning Phases

We offer flexible, hourly-rate packages to match your project's needs.

Phase 1: Rough Clean

$30 / hour

Perfect for the end of the framing and drywall stage.

  • Sweeping and clearing of all floors and baseboards.
  • Removal of debris, nails, small wood scraps, and trash.
  • Scraping spackling, caulk, and glue from floors and tubs.

Phase 2: Detail Clean

$45 / hour

The most popular choice for a nearly-finished project.

  • Comprehensive dusting: walls, ceilings, fixtures, and vents.
  • Detailed wipe-down of baseboards, trim, and door frames.
  • Window frames, sills, and tracks cleaned (stickers removed).
  • Inside and out cleaning of all cabinets, shelves, and counters.

Phase 3: Final Polish

$55 / hour

The "white glove" service that makes the property shine.

  • Includes all Phase 1 & 2 services, PLUS:
  • Thorough sanitization of kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Cleaning and polishing of all fixtures and hardware.
  • Detailed vacuuming and final, streak-free mopping.
